How can I help?

We’re building a student-focused app and looking for people with experience who can help bring our prototype to life. Skills that would be especially useful include:

  • React & React Native ... building mobile apps
  • JavaScript / TypeScript ... front-end and app logic
  • Firebase or other backends ... storing and managing data
  • UI/UX design & Figma ... designing smooth, user-friendly interfaces
  • Marketing, growth, and community building ... helping us reach students
  • Project management / product design ... organizing features and workflows
  • Anything else related to app development ... if you have experience, we want to hear from you!

If you, or someone in your family or network, has experience in any of these areas, we’d love to collaborate or get mentorship.
